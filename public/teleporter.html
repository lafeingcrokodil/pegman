Would you like to choose a mysterious location for Pegman to teleport to?

If so, please proceed as follows:

  1. Open Google Maps.
  2. Click on the little yellow Pegman icon at the bottom right and drag it over the map. A bunch of blue lines should appear.
  3. Drop Pegman on one of the blue lines, wherever you'd like. That should open a Google Street View panorama.
  4. Move around until you're satisfied with the exact location.
  5. Copy the URL that's in the address bar of your browser.
  6. Paste the URL into the input field below and press ENTER.

All set! The Pegman Locator URL has been generated and copied to your clipboard. You can now share it with whomever you'd like to challenge to find Pegman's location.
